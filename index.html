About Us

We are students from SI 47 International Class at Telkom University and we have a clear vision and mission that we're really proud of. We aim to lead in the field of information system development and with top notch education and ongoing innovation, we strive to be pioneers of positive change in the industry and society.

Our vision is we want to inspire and be educated to become agents of change. We commit to high standard learning and bring innovations that truly impact, both in the business world and socially.

What we strive for in our mission:

  • We learn the latest and best in information systems, going beyond global standards.
  • We use the latest technology and creative teaching methods to make our learning more interactive and interesting.
  • We strengthen our global network by collaborating with universities and industries from around the world.
  • We encourage each other to create innovations that can change the way we live and work.
  • We are ready to lead and inspire positive change wherever we are, following our motto, 'Be The Change.'
